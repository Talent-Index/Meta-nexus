The Orbitians
In the vast realms of Metanexus, countless beings exist, each with their own mysteries. Among them, the most advanced and noble are the Orbitians. High above the clouds, the Orbitians sail the skies in colossal metal sky-machines. They are explorers, dreamers, and guardians — a race that balances between Earth and the heavens, never fully belonging to either. For generations, the Orbitians have been locked in conflict with a race known as the Upside-Downs. These strange beings, with their inverted bodies and ground-bound existence, see the skies as forbidden territory. Consumed by envy and desperation, the Upside-Downs believe that if they could breach the Orbitians’ world, they would finally tip the war in their favor. Their plan is cunning — instead of striking directly, they turn their attention to humans. To the Upside-Downs, humanity represents a potential key, a bridge into Orbitian territory. By controlling or deceiving humans, they hope to gain the one thing they have never had: a way into the skies. The fate of the Metanexus may depend on whether humans choose to ally with the Orbitians… or fall into the hands of the Upside-Downs.
